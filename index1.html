History Of VSICS

The Dr. Virendra Swarup Educational Foundation (VSEF) in Uttar Pradesh was founded in 1989 in memory of Dr. Virendra Swarup, an influential educationist. One of its components is the Dr. Virendra Swarup Institute of Computer Studies (VSICS), which is the first self-financed institute in Kanpur to offer computer education in the form of MCA. VSEF also manages 10+2 schools in various cities of Uttar Pradesh. The foundation is affiliated with Dayanand Siksha Sansthan, overseeing more than 25 postgraduate and intermediate colleges across Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand. VSICS has gained recognition for its MCA, MBA, BCA, and BBA programs, designed to nurture talent in the IT and Management fields. The institute is ISO 9001:2000 certified, emphasizing its commitment to providing quality education over its 25-year history.
