Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a two years long professional post-graduate programme for candidates wanting to delve deeper into the world of computer application development with the help of learning modern programming language. The programme is a blend of both theoretical and practical knowledge. An MCA degree endows studentsâ€™ an opportunity to work with tools meant to develop better and faster applications.
